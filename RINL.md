### **REVISED RINL VOLUNTARY RETIREMENT SCHEME**

#### **1.0 Title:**
The Scheme will hereinafter be known as **"RINL Voluntary Retirement Scheme"**.

#### **2.0 Objective:**
To rationalize the existing manpower, achieve optimum human resource utilization, cost reduction and improved productivity.

#### **3.0 Scope:**
The scheme shall apply to regular/ permanent employees (Executives including Non-unionised supervisors and Non-Executives) of RINL except the following:
> i. Employee appointed on Fixed Tenure/Contract basis  
> ii. Government Servants and others employed on deputation terms in RINL,  
> iii. Employees on Lien; and  
> iv. Employees under 'Bond' consequent to training/study leave, sponsorship.  
> v. Casual/Contract labour

#### **4.0 Eligibility:**
Employees, who have completed minimum **15 years of service** in RINL and have attained **45 years of age** will be eligible for applying for VRS. Eligibility will be reckoned on the date of application of the employee, opting VRS.

#### **5.0 Definitions:**
> **a. "Appellate Committee"** means the Committee constituted for the purpose of hearing appeal of an employee, in case his application of Voluntary Retirement is rejected by the Screening Committee.  
> **b. "Basic Pay"** means, Basic Pay + Stagnation Increment (SI) + PP (if any).  
> **c. "Board"** means, RINL Board.  
> **d. "Competent Authority"** means the authority/ authorities empowered to grant VR under the scheme.  
> **e. "Company"** means Rashtriya Ispat Nigam Limited.  
> **f. "Employee"** means a person in the regular employment of RINL in a prescribed pay scale.  
> **g. "Salary"** means Basic Pay and Dearness Allowance only.  
> **h. "Screening Committee"** means committee constituted for examining the applications of VR received from employees.  
> **i. "Scheme"** means RINL Voluntary Retirement Scheme.  
> **j. "Voluntary Retirement"** means option provided by the Company and accepted by the employee to switch into retirement before normal date of Superannuation.

#### **6.0 Benefits under the Scheme**
An employee whose offer for voluntary retirement is accepted will be entitled to the following benefits:

> **6.1 Voluntary Retirement (VR) Compensation (Ex-gratia):** An employee will be entitled to VR compensation as per Gujarat Pattern.  
> **6.1.1 Gujarat Pattern:** The VR Compensation shall consist of salary of **35 days for every completed year of service** and **25 days for the leftover service till superannuation**. The compensation will be subject to a minimum of **Rs. 25,000 (Rupees Twenty Five thousand) or 250 days salary whichever is higher**. However, this total compensation shall not exceed the sum of the salary that the employee would have drawn at the prevailing level for the leftover service. The VR compensation amount shall be paid on lump sum basis.  
> **6.1.2 //Deleted//**  
> **6.1.3 Salary for the purpose of VR compensation** will consist of Basic Pay and DA only being drawn by an employee as on the date of release.  
> **6.1.4 The salary for VR compensation** shall be calculated on the basis of 30 days in a month.  
> **6.1.5 For the purpose of calculation,** the completed year of service and also the service rendered during a part of the year will be taken into account. For the service rendered during a part of the year, the compensation will be calculated on pro-rata basis.  
> **6.2 Provident Fund and Gratuity payment** shall be as admissible under the respective rules of RINL and statutory provisions.  
> **6.3 Leave Encashment** for the un-availed balance portion of EL and HPL as per the extant rules of the RINL on Superannuation. Encashment of Casual Leave on pro rata upto the date of release of the employee.  
> **6.4 Medical Facilities** as extended in case of Superannuation.  
> **6.5 Transfer Benefits** shall be extended in terms of 'Travelling Allowance Rules and will be considered as applicable on superannuation.  
> **6.6 Employees shall be presented with Service Certificate.**  
> **6.7 Employee shall be eligible for benefits** under RINL Employees (Defined Contribution) Pension Scheme, subject to fulfillment of other criteria.  
> **6.8 Enhanced VR compensation** on account of any increase in wages/ salary consequent to wage/salary revision will be payable; subject to terms and conditions of the wage/salary revision and the applicable DPE guidelines.  
> **6.9 For reckoning of benefits,** date of release shall be the relevant date.

#### **7.0 Repayment of Loans/ Advances:**
Employee opting for VR shall have to repay outstanding loans/ advances with accrued interest till the date of retirement. The same may be adjusted from VR compensation/ leave Encashment/gratuity, payable to the employee.

#### **8.0 Notice Pay:**
> • Employee opting for VR shall be given notice period of **30 days** by the Company. Notice pay equivalent to 30 days pay shall be paid if the due notice is not given.  
> • If the application of an employee for voluntary retirement is accepted instantaneously and payment is arranged by the Management on the same day, the concerned individual would be entitled to payment of ex gratia along with the notice period pay. However, the payment of ex-gratia for service rendered or left over service before superannuation as well as the amount payable for the notice period **should not exceed the Basic Pay plus DA** that would have been paid to the employee who has opted for voluntary retirement till the date of his superannuation.  
> • Where the Management takes time to take a decision about the acceptance of an application submitted by the employee for voluntary retirement and allows the notice period to lapse or the individual concerned has drawn full salary during the notice period served by him, notice period pay will not be admissible.

#### **9.0 Tax Payment:**
Income Tax liability, if any will be deducted from the benefits admissible to the employee, as per the Income Tax Act, 1961.

#### **10.0 Re-employment:**
> **10.1** An employee availing of VR under this scheme will **not be eligible for re-employment in RINL or any of its subsidiaries or Joint Ventures**.  
> **10.2** Once, an employee avails VR from RINL, he will not be allowed to take up employment in other PSU. In case, he desires to do so, he shall return the VR compensation received by him to RINL.

#### **11.0 Procedure:**
> **11.1** Application of VR shall be submitted by the employee to the concerned HR Establishment zone through proper channel.  
> **11.2** After scrutiny and necessary clearances, the HR establishment shall submit the application to the Nodal Officer, nominated for the purpose of the scheme.  
> **11.3** Nodal Officer shall submit the same to the Screening Committee after due verification.  
> **11.4** The Screening Committee shall examine and shall submit its recommendation for accepting/rejecting.  
> **11.5** Cases recommended shall be put for the approval of the Competent Authority. Once approved by the Competent Authority, VR application cannot be withdrawn.  
> **11.6** Employee whose VR application has been rejected by the Screening Committee may appeal before the Appellate Committee within **3 working days** of receiving the communication for rejection.  
> **11.7** The Appellate Committee shall examine the appeal and dispose of the same on merit.  
> **11.8** Effective date for releasing of the employee on VR shall be the **last working day of the month**.  
> **11.9** An employee who is granted VR will have to be clear from all disciplinary and vigilance angles.

#### **12.0 Screening Committee:**
The Screening Committee will be constituted for examining VR applications and making recommendation to the Approving Authority. The Screening Committee shall consist of the following:

| Grades of the Employee opting for VRS | Members of Screening committee |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Non-Executives and Executives upto E-4 grade** | i. HoD concerned not below the rank of E-7 grade <br> ii. HR Representative concerned not below the rank of E-6 grade <br> iii. MS Representative, not below the rank of E-4 grade <br> **HR Representative will be the Convener.** |
| **E-5 to E-7** | i. Head of the concerned Division not below the rank of E-9 grade <br> ii. HR Representative concerned not below the rank of E-7 grade <br> iii. MS Representative, not below the rank of E-6 grade <br> **HR Representative will be the Convener.** |
| **E-8 & E-9** | i. Concerned Functional Director <br> ii. HR Representative concerned not below the rank of E-7/E-8 grade <br> iii. MS Representative, not below the rank of E-6 grade <br> **HR Representative will be the Convener.** |

Screening Committee will be constituted with the approval of CMD. The Screening Committee shall examine the applications as per the Operating procedure as given at Annexure.

#### **13.0 Appellate Committee:**
**13.1** Employee whose VR application has been rejected by the Screening Committee may appeal before the Appellate Committee. The level of Appellate Committee is above Screening Committee.
